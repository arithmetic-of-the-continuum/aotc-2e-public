AotC is a mathematical text that bridges the gap between undergraduate and graduate mathematics. The book builds systematically from first principles to advanced topics. AotC arose from a recognition that while many excellent mathematical texts exist, they often either skip crucial foundational details or become specialized too quickly. The aim is to provide a coherent narrative.

AotC assumes the mathematical maturity level of an intermediate to late undergraduate, but is fairly self-contained, so that early undergraduates and precocious high school students can also follow along.
